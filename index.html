OUR MENUS

Our creative, elevated food and beverage program combines satisfying staples with imaginative twists. From boozy brunches and happy hours to family dinners, special occasions and everything in between, The Original has something for everyone.

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are a fan favorite when visiting coffee shops. This is especially the case because of the latte art decorations that are often drawn into the steamed milk foam.

Espresso

Espresso is not necessarily a type of coffee, but a way to make coffee. The difference is the amount of water used to make espressos versus regularly brewed coffee

Mocha

Mocha, which is actually short for mochaccino, is another popular coffee variation, right up there with a latte, and for a good reason.

Affogato

Affogato technically is not a beverage, but it is still coffee-based. Affogato is actually a dessert that is meant to either be slowly sipped or enjoyed with a spoon..

Frappuccino

Frappuccinos are often compared to milkshakes, which is understandable. A Frappuccino is basically just an iced cappuccino that was blended into a milkshake.
